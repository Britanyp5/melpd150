Creative Statement

My name is Britany Palaguachi, and I am a full-time student, part-time worker, and 25/8 mother. Getting older and having different experiences has made me rethink life itself — to romanticize it and look deeper into it. I like dancing Ecuadorian traditional dances, which has allowed me to connect to my Ecuadorian roots. Going to college has opened my mind to different ways of thinking. Helping run a restaurant has helped me understand the business world and socialize with others. Being a mother has reinforced my love for life because it reminds me every moment is precious. A good way of keeping those memories intact is taking pictures. I used to hate taking pictures — I thought it was a waste of storage on my phone. But now I pay for extra storage just to capture everything my toddler does.
